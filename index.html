📘 How to Play

🎯 HOW TO PLAY BINGO


1. Each player gets a 5×5 grid filled with numbers (1–25 in random order).


2. Players take turns calling a number.
→ If the number exists on your board, it gets marked.


3. Your goal is to complete lines:
✔ Horizontal
✔ Vertical
✔ Diagonal


4. Each completed line = 1 point.


5. First player to complete 5 lines shouts "BINGO!" and wins.


⚠️ Strategy Tip:
Choose numbers that help you complete multiple lines at once!
